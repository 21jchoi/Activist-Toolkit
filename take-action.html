Take Action

Invoke Change

You can help lower the rate of sexual assault on college campuses. Despite the shockingly high rates of sexual assault on campus, colleges continue to overlook this issue. In order to create change, we must make our voices heard and prove that this is an ongoing problem on many college campuses. Signing petitions is a great way to bring attention to an issue. Websites like Change.org and iPetitions hold thousands of petitions that you can sign quickly and for free. You can also donate to non-profit organizations that work to support victims of sexual assault and campaign to reform school policies protecting the students.

Step By Step

Once you have completed an item, check it off the list!

  • Go to change.org and look up petitions with keywords "campus sexual assault" or similar words.
  • Browse the petitions and sign some! If you need a place to start, here's one petition: Designated Supoort for Sexual Assault Victims on Campuses
  • Click here to donate to the nonprofit organization RAINN, the nation's largest anti-sexual violence organization.
  • Share this website on social media to raise awareness!
